GPX for waypoints of Labels by IOW Parish

So far I have found only 2 android apps that can display waypoint labels contained in these gpx files. 1) OSMAnd which will display the label along with a marker, or custom marker and 2) AlpineQuest Pro which can show the label of the marker only.
